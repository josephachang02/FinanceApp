Personal Spending Expenses


Our personal lifestyle choices largely dictate how much of our personal expenses get pulled from. And although these expenses don't amount nearly to what our primary expenses total to, a large majority of people unknowingly over exceed this amount. However, this portion of our finance is where we also have the most control over, unlike our housing or child care expenses where we don't have as much control over. This section will pool all of our total Personal Expenses together to give you an idea for how much of your spending percentage comes from personal spending.
